Chatbot for Gifting E-commerce website

Our Gifting E-commerce Chatbot transforms the gift selection process by providing personalized, AI-driven recommendations tailored to users' needs. By collecting essential details such as occasion, recipient, budget, and interests, the chatbot curates relevant gift options with ease. Integrated sentiment analysis helps understand emotional tones, offering gifts that align with both practical and emotional preferences. The interactive star-rating system speeds up the process by allowing users to quickly rate their preferences. Offering both experience-based and product-based gifts, the chatbot ensures users find meaningful options for any occasion. Additionally, AI-suggested greeting cards can be personalized, adding an extra thoughtful touch. The chatbot combines cutting-edge AI with real-time e-commerce integration to bring convenience, efficiency, and emotional resonance to modern gifting.
